Case Hardened

The case hardened isn't usually classified ad a high tier knife, but with the right pattern it could go for loads of keys or digital money.

Case hardened knives have many patterns, ranging from dirty rust to metaalic sky blue, the pattern for case hardend knives are quite easy to identify. The rarity and price of a Case Hardened knife(or gun) is determined by the amount of blue on the knife (or gun), the more the blue, the more expensive and rare it is.

Blue Gems

The most wanted pattern on a Case Hardened Skin is a Blue Gem, a Blue Gem is a pattern in which there is more than 80% of the skin covered in blue, people are willing to pay ALOT of money for these.

Molten Gold

This isn't as sought out as the Blue Gems, though still popular. The Molten Gold is basically a golden pattern on the skin, sometimes Molten Golds have a bit of rust and are mistaken for Silver. Sometimes full gold Case Hardened skins go for a sweet amount.

Rainbows

Rainbows are the third rarest type of Case Hardened skins, these skins have blobs of blue with a nice amount of purple. I personally like Rainbow because the mixture of Blue, Gold and Rainbow makes the skin catchy and unique.

Silver / Rust

Silver / Rust is the least sought out pattern for case hardened. This pattern usually looks unpainted, if not colorless it has close to no color. Sometimes they have small spots of gold or blue, but usually not visible.
